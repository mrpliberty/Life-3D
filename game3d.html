3D Game of Life Rules

Grid: 3D cubic lattice. Each cell has 26 neighbors (the surrounding 3×3×3 cube minus itself).

Classic 3D Ruleset (B3/S23):

  • Birth: A dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes alive.
  • Survival: A live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbors survives.
  • Death: All other live cells die (underpopulation or overpopulation).

Extended Ruleset (B0-26):

  • Any cell with any number of neighbors can survive/born (chaotic mode).

Interaction:

  • Click a cube to toggle it
  • Drag to orbit camera
  • Scroll to zoom
  • Right-drag to pan
